StravaSTADIUM RUN CLUB - Sverige
STADIUM RUN CLUB - STOCKHOLM X PUMA TTRC
Kungsgatan 8 111 43 Stockholm Sverige

Stockholm 📍 Stadium Run Club Torsdag 2/7 Kl: 17.30 - 18.30 Hejsan! 👋 Nu är anmälan uppe för nästa veckas Run Club! Ett perfekt tillfälle för dig som vill springa tillsammans med andra 🏃🏼‍♀️🏃🏽‍♂️. Passen är utformade så att alla kan vara med och utmana sig själva på sina egna villkor och självklart är alla välkomna oavsett tidigare löpvana. - Uppvärmning - Löpskolning - Intervaller - Nedjogg Möts inne i butiken på Kungsgatan 8 📍 Join us for a run with PUMA and triple threshold RC. Get the chance to test the new Deviate pure by PUMA. All runners will receive an exclusive event t shirt, and we’ll have snacks and drinks waiting for you. Expect a session inspired by community, music and running, plus a few surprises along the way. We will be running 5 km at a 5:30 pace/km, with limited spots (60 runners max) BONUS: Two runners will win a pair of Deviate pure! Genom att anmäla mig godkänner jag Stadiums integritetspolicy, att mina uppgifter hanteras i enlighet med GDPR och att få mejlkommunikation kopplat till eventet. Stadiums integritetspolicy hittar du här. http://www.stadium.se/privacy Ses på torsdag 👏

StravaGordito Athletic Group
GORDITO: 4TH OF JULY 4K SHOWDOWN
Häktet, Stockholm, Sweden

Limited entries are now open. Teams of four. Four bars, four kilometres, a pint and a shot at each — every member, no exceptions. Nobody runs on until the last glass is down. Pick your four best and enter a team. The slowest of you sets the pace, so choose carefully. Not running? Even better. Spectators can place bets on the teams — back a side, study the form, lose your money like a patriot. Live cheer zone at Häktet, with camera feeds from all four bars up on the big LED screen so the family can watch you lose in real time. Warm-up from 13:00. Racing starts at 14:00 sharp. Start and finish at Häktet. Saturday, 4th July. Independence optional; the hangover isn't. Team RSVP at https://gordito.world/4k

Weekly runMikkeller Running Club Stockholm
Go for a run, grab a beer!
Mikkeller Bar, Östgötagatan 33

Our normal routes are 5km, 7km and 9km. The pace for 5km is around 6:30 minutes/km. The 9km we often do in two pace groups. Meet up outside the bar at 17:55, we start running at 18:00. You can leave your bag upstairs before the run. If you got a cold or any other symptoms, please stay at home. Welcome!
